Our Story

We started with a simple belief: design should cut through noise

Founded in 2012, Wedgezone was born from the conviction that great design isn't just how something looks — it's how it works, how it feels, and what it says about who you are.

We've grown from a two-person studio into a team of 40+ strategists, designers, and technologists. Our work spans global brands and ambitious startups — all united by a shared obsession with doing things well.
